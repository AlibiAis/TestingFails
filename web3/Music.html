Hard to imagine was released alongside the band’s self-titled third studio album The Neighbourhood. Whilst “Scary Love”, “Sadderdaze”, “You Get Me So High” and “Stuck with Me” made it onto the album from the previous EPs Hard and To Imagine, these tracks did not. They are, however, included in the deluxe edition.
